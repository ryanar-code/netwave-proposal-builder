Executive Summary
A social media audit is a comprehensive analysis of your current social media efforts to determine what's working, what's not, and how to optimize your strategies for better results. This proposal outlines the steps and benefits of conducting a thorough social media audit for [Client Name].
Upon completion of the audit, Netwave will present a full recap report of the areas analyzed as well as a social media strategy for improved results.

Scope of Work

Landscape & Analysis
Overview
Netwave will perform a deep dive into [company’s] current social media channels and tactics including:
	•	Thorough performance analysis on [LIST OF SOCIAL CHANNELS] 
	•	Current posting schedule
	•	Audit of graphics
	•	Effective tagging results
	•	Identify top-performing and underperforming content
	•	Address specific road blocks or opportunities for improvement

Competitor Landscape
Analyze the social media presence of [# OF COMPETITORS] main competitors. The competitor analysis includes:
	•	Total amount of followers, overall posting engagement, and content strategies by channel
	•	Samples of top performing posts from competitors by channel
	•	Social advertising strategy of top competitors if applicable 


Proposed Strategy
Strategic Framework
Based on the completed analysis, Netwave will present an all encompassing strategy for an effective social media campaign including:
	•	Defining primary and secondary social media goals
	•	Defining target audiences and prospective clients
	•	Recommend custom strategies and tactics per social platform; paid and organic
	•	Content direction
	•	Content format recommendations for more robust campaign
	•	Define primary and secondary key points of interest and success measurement tactics

Content Strategy
Netwave will provide content suggestions to be used on each social media channel. Direction includes the following per channel [LIST OF SOCIAL CHANNELS] :
	•	Content goals
	•	Define content buckets to distribute to current followers. For example:
	•	Service Highlights
	•	Testimonials
	•	Partners / collaboration opportunities
	•	Industry perspective / sought after information
	•	Target Audience
	•	Cadence recommendations

Content Enhancement Suggestions
Upon completion of a full competitor analysis, Netwave will organize and present a series of suggestions to assist [Client Name] outperform their defined competition on social media. 
These custom content enhancement suggestions can be defined as a guide to be referenced and followed to have optimal success on social media channels.  

Defined Deliverables
Upon completing the social media audit, [Client Name] will receive:
	•	Comprehensive Audit Report outlining findings, recommendations, and actionable insights 
	•	Current Landscape: analysis of what’s working and where new opportunities lie
	•	Competitor Analysis: A summary of key takeaways from the analysis of competitors' social media strategies. Includes examples of competitor posts.
	•	Campaign Insights: Suggestions for the overall strategy, audience and channel targeting, advertising suggestions, recommendations to challenge competitors, optimizing content types, posting frequency, and engagement strategies.
	•	Refined Strategy: Based on the defined company goals, an all-encompassing strategy to move forward. 

Benefits
Informed decisions guided by data insights pave the way for more effective strategies. This, in turn, enhances engagement rates by leveraging optimized content and personalized approaches. Additionally, gaining insights into competitor strategies facilitates better differentiation and sparks innovation. The process involves skillfully crafting content that deeply connects with the target audience, ultimately resulting in elevated conversion rates.
To achieve these outcomes, a clear roadmap for refining social media strategies is essential, ensuring the attainment of measurable goals. Crucially, it involves defining overarching company objectives and formulating a strategic path towards their realization. Integral to this process is the creation of a robust measurement framework, tracking success in terms of the defined goals and return on investment.
